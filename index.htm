Certifications

Python certificates validate proficiency in the language and are awarded by educational institutions or online platforms, signifying competence in coding and application development. They are recognized credentials in the software and IT industry.
The HackerRank Problem Solving Certificate is earned by individuals showcasing exceptional problem-solving skills through coding challenges on the platform. This certification validates their ability to tackle algorithmic and coding problems effectively.
The NPTEL Computer Architecture certificate is a recognition of proficiency in understanding and designing computer systems, awarded by the National Programme on Technology Enhanced Learning (NPTEL). It validates expertise in concepts such as processor architecture, memory systems, and parallel processing.
